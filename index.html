About Elektrikerr

Trusted Electrical Expertise Since 2009

Elektrikerr was founded with a single mission: to provide honest, high-quality electrical services that clients can trust. Over 15 years, we have grown from a single-person operation into a team of fully qualified, NICEIC-registered electricians serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Every project we take on is completed to the highest standards of safety and workmanship. We take the time to understand your needs, explain our approach clearly, and deliver results that stand the test of time.

  • NICEIC registered and fully insured for your peace of mind
  • All work certified and compliant with current wiring regulations
  • Transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden charges
  • 24/7 emergency response for urgent electrical issues
